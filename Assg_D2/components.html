Best Restaurants In Pune

Vohuman Cafe

Vohuman Cafe is up and running at 6am, and serves everything from Cheese Omelettes and Bun Maskas to the childhood favourite Bournvita and of course, Irani Chai. Plan ahead if you want to make it here for breakfast—there is a long queue and you will most likely have to wait.

Cafe Goodluck

Don’t let the archaic charm and mirrors in this dusty corner cafe fool you. It’s been a favourite in Pune for over eight decades now. While it’s bun-maska is the known-famous, we’ll let you onto another secret – their keema pav and chicken tikka sandwich are a hit.

Cafe
Yezdan

Situated in Pune’s Parsi hub. The look and feel of this cafe may fool you for a setting from a Bollywood film from the 90s. You may not want to miss out on their sali scrambled eggs double and anda bhurji. The bhurji is served with special garlic chutney, that keeps you coming back!

Irani
Cafe

From Ardeshir’s soda to Chicken berry pulao, there’s no Irani dish that this cafe doesn’t serve! Their Joojeh kebabs will give you foodporn. The warmth and energy in this cafe along with the unique spices the traditional Parsi dishes are sure to make you feel at peace, even in the middle of a stressful day.

Shisha Jazz Cafe

If you’re looking for a slightly posh, cafe-ish setting for a date or a good afternoon/evening with your friends and family, Shisha Jazz Cafe should be your ideal joint. But don’t go only on the decor. The food here is as fancy as the interiors. Our best choice here was the Soup-e-jou and mutton Rogangosh.

Diamond
Queen

Another hidden gem in Camp, this one has to be your go-to place if you love chicken and mutton chops! A small little place in a bustling area, this place deserves some credit for why Pune loves chicken lollipop as well. The cottage-ish look of the place will add to your experience of a yummy meal!
